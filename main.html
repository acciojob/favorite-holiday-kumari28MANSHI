Goa, India


Goa is a famous holiday destination known for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, Portuguese architecture, and delicious seafood.

  1. Baga Beach – popular for water sports.
  2. Fort Aguada – historic sea fort.
  3. Dudhsagar Falls – breathtaking waterfall.
  4. Basilica of Bom Jesus – famous church.
  5. Anjuna Flea Market – lively shopping area.
  • Goan Fish Curry – spicy seafood dish.
  • Prawn Balchão – tangy prawn pickle.
  • Chicken Xacuti – coconut-based curry.
  • Bebinca – traditional dessert.
  • Feni – local Goan drink.
